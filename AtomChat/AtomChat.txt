AtomChat
Version 1

AtomChat adds real-time chat to your FogBugz site. Your developers won't waste time emailing each other. 

AtomChat integrates seamlessly with your FogBugz site and adds dozens of new features including: 

- Instant text chat 
- Voice/video chat 
- Collaborative document sharing 
- Screen-sharing 
- File transfer and a lot more! 

Simply visit http://www.atomchat.com and register to start using AtomChat with your site! 

Installation takes only seconds and works out-of-the-box for all your developers.

This version requires FogBugz 7.3 or higher.
