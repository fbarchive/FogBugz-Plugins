FogBugzTimeSheetPlugin
version 1.1

Provides extended reports from the WorkingOn timesheet information

This plugin uses the information from the "WorkingOn" timeinterval and creates two reports - TimeSheetReport showing hours by person per project. 
StatusReport - Showing BugEvents created in selected period. 

Documentation is available on our help site: 
http://help.fogcreek.com/8907/timesheet-reports#fogbugztimesheet

This version requires FogBugz 7.3 or higher.
