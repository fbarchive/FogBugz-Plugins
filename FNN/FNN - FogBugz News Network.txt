FNN - FogBugz News Network (Extended Reporting)

The FNN FogBugz plugin provides additional reporting functionality to FogBugz to better enable you to keep tabs on your projects. 

It also adds a working on indicator to the case-view.

This version requires FogBugz 8.3.42 or higher.
